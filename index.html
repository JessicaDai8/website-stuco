About Me

Hello, I am a current freshman Computer Science Student at CMU pursuing a B.S degree. I am passionate for learning and teaching, especially in math and CS. Feel free to ask me anything!
I am a 21-241 Supplemental Instruction leader because I am truly passionate for teaching and have experience working with students. I host bi-weekly review sessions where I prepare and present problems for students to tackle. I also meet with the professors to discuss exam content and answer questions on Piazza/Canvas. I want my students to genuinely understand and engage in the content as they progress through the course. So far, this experience has been very rewarding to me as my students have done well on exams but have also expressed genuine interest in the content. I believe this passion for learning is what really matters.
